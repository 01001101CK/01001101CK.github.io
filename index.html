Design + Development

I believe web design can be more diverse and mindful. We are influenced by our environments, so we need to be mindful of how we make an impact on our users.

User Experience Journey Map

User experience research is essential to understand how customers use and experience your product, you should try to see things from their perspective. The customer journey is the series of steps and interactions customers go through when they encounter your company and its products. This includes everything from their initial encounter with your product to their final purchase. This process is crucial to design maintainable, scalable and performant code.

System Design/Coding
